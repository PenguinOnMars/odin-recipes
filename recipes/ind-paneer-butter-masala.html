Paneer Butter Masala

Ingredients

  • Cashews
    • helps thicken gravy
    • soak in hot water for an hour, peel off the skin
  • Paneer
    • no starch
    • soak for 15-20 minutes, prevents rubbery paneer
  • Onions
  • Ripe tomatoes
    • if unripe AVOID
  • Butter
    • unsalted
  • Cream
    • fresh cream, whipping cream, heavy cream, cooking cream & sterilized cream
