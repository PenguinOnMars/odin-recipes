Paneer Butter Masala

Ingredients

Gravy

  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 2 green cardamoms (elaichi)
  • 12-14 cashews
  • 1 cup onion cubed - 3 medium
  • ¾ teaspoon salt - adjust to taste
  • ¾ teaspoon sugar - optional
  • ¾-1 teaspoon red chilli powder - kashmiri or any low heat kind, adjust for best color
  • 1½ cups tomatoes, finely chopped - 3 large
  • ¾-1 teaspoon garam masala powder - adjust to taste
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder (daniya powder) - optional
